Projects

Data science, economics, and spatial analytics projects that apply quantitative methods to real-world problems.

School Zone Accident Analysis

A geospatial analysis of collision data from SANDAG and the City of San Diego. I geolocated collision points, created 1-mile buffers around schools, and performed spatial joins to associate accidents with schools and nearby roads. Using ArcGIS, I identified hotspot areas with the most accidents during peak school hours (7–9 a.m., 2–4 p.m.).

Skills: ArcGIS, Spatial Join, Hotspot Analysis, Data Visualization

Survey Sampling & Weighting

Designed and executed two traditional survey sampling projects, including population frame development, probabilistic sample selection, and contractor coordination. Applied organizational weighting with poststratification, raking, and machine learning bias correction (XGBoost) to ensure data representativeness and analytic rigor.

Skills: R, SQL, XGBoost, Poststratification, Raking, Survey Methodology

Labor Market & Economic Analysis

Conducted empirical analyses on employment and wage data using public datasets from the Bureau of Labor Statistics and Census Bureau. Developed time-series and cross-sectional models to evaluate workforce patterns, skills demand, and regional economic mobility.

Skills: Econometrics, R, SQL, Data Cleaning, Visualization, Regression Analysis

Predictive Modeling for Program Outcomes

Built predictive models to estimate program outcomes and identify key performance drivers across organizational datasets. Applied regression, random forest, and gradient boosting to optimize resource allocation and improve accuracy in decision-making.

Skills: Python, R, scikit-learn, Feature Engineering, Model Evaluation

Data Engineering with Databricks

Used Advana/Databricks to process, transform, and analyze large structured datasets. Implemented SQL-based pipelines for cleaning, merging, and feature extraction to support econometric and operational models.

Skills: Databricks, SQL, PySpark, Data Pipelines, Cloud Analytics
